Pomo - What?

---

This website is based off of the Pomodoro Technique which is a time management method developed by Francesco Cirillo in the late 1980s. The technique uses a timer to break down work into intervals traditionally 25 minutes in length, separated by short breaks. These intervals are known as "pomodori", the plural of the Italian word pomodoro for "tomato".

---

The idea is that for each successive interval of work, you get a timed game break before you head back to work.

HOWEVER

For every successive interval of work you accomplish, the reward multipliyer in game increases.

---

The longer you keep your work habit, the better the rewards!
